About Us

Founded in 2010, Vicious Cycles has since become one of the most popular and respected Bike shops in the Raleigh area. Our Community-oriented business model puts the customers' needs before anything else, as we truly strive for a personal and inclusive buying process.

In 2021, Vicious Cycles was awarded "Best Bicycle Company" by the city of Raleigh. Since then, we have yet to provide a lesser service to our customers.

As an active part of the community, Vicious Cycles is always hosting and participating in local sports events. If you would like to partner with us for a future event, please get in contact at viciouscycles@gmail.com
